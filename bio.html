Artist Bio

Dokyong Ryoo

About Dokyong is a digital artist and data scientist based in Chicago, Illinois. Born in Seoul and raised across Seoul, Taipei, and Chicago, her work draws on the intersections of place, memory, and digital systems. Working primarily with video, internet art, and web technologies including CSS, HTML, and JavaScript, she creates digital archives that document and interrogate how places are remembered, lost, and reconstructed in online spaces.
Education Dokyong is currently pursuing a degree in Data Science and a minor in Media Arts and Design at the University of Chicago, where her practice continues to evolve at the intersection of analytical and creative methods.
